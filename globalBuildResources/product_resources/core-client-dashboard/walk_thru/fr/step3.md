**Now you need a checking document**.

- Click *New document* on the dashboard and select *tCore checks*. Follow the steps to link your translation.
- Open your new checking document. You may be prompted to download some needed resources. Download them all.
- You can now click *Add checks for book* at will.
- **If you have tCore 3 work to import**.
    - In tCore 3, click *Export as zip* on each book.
    - In tCore 4, click *Import tC3 Book* for each book.

Great! Click a book to see progress and start checking!

Note that the checks contain a snapshot of your translation, meaning edits made during checking will not directly change the translation document itself. You can merge back these changes later.
